AI in healthcare

The aim of the project is to create a small, accessible booklet in a zine format illustrating the key ideas behind the use of data, AI, decision-support for dengue with a LMIC focus and all the challenges which come with it. We want to touch on specific topics which might include bias, accountability, responsible innovation in a resource-limited context and data use. At the end perhaps a small checklist or call to action for important / interesting points to consider.

Presented at:

  • International Congress on Infectious Diseases (November 2022, Kuala Lumpur)
  • Great Exhibition Road Festival (March 2022, London)
